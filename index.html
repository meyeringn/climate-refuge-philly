About the project A civic tool for a hotter, less predictable city. Climate Refuge Philly is an independent prototype designed at the intersection of disability access, public transit, and climate resilience. The next version will replace demonstration locations with verified Philadelphia data and add a clear correction process. Climate Refuge Philly Independent civic technology prototype. Made with care for disabled Philadelphians, neighbors, caregivers, and anyone trying to stay safe.
